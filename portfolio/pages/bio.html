About Me

My name is Nathan More and I am a game developer. I’m currently studying Game Design & Interactive Media at UC Irvine. When I’m not working on my programming projects, you can find me playing video games with friends, having dinner and a board game night with my family, or watching sit coms with my fiancée.

    Fun Facts:
  • I was born and raised in Southern California but had the opportunity to move around a lot within the area.
  • I have an associates degree in both Computer Science and Mathematics.
  • My favorite aspects of video games are immersion and progression. As such, my favorite video game genre is action RPG, though I am really a generalist and enjoying playing many different types of games.
  • Some of my favorite games of all time are The Witcher 3, Dishonored 1 & 2, and Ark: Survival Evolved. Though there are many others from a variety of genres that I love dearly.
  • I also enjoy board games and tabletop roleplaying games such as D&D. I even created my own rules-lite fantasy TTRPG that I play with friends.
  • I am the current Design Director for the Video Game Development Club where I lead the Design Department's operations and host workshops for the club on various design topics.
  • Anyone who knows me well is aware of my infactuation with pizza in all forms, and my cruel affliction of lactose intolerance which keeps me from it most of the time.
