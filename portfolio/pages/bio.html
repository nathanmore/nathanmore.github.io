About Me

I have grown up in Southern California and have lived in the area for all of my life, often moving around. I have played games for my entire life. It has helped me make friends, grow relationships, and have fun. I've always been interested in making games because I want to make new experiences for myself and others to enjoy. After High School, I attended community college studying computer science. During that time, I discovered the Game Design and Interactive Media major at UC Irvine.

After graduating with an Associates in Computer Science and Math, I transferred into the UCI program where I am currently studying. Since coming to UCI I have learned so much about game programming, design, and the games inustry as a whole. I have worked on many projects with my peers and am continuing to work on more. I have joined the Video Game Development Club (VGDC) and was recently appointed as the Design Director.
